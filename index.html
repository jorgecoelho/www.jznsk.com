JZNSK Ltd is an independent professional services company providing advisory, research, and delivery support across technology, leadership, and organisational systems.

The company supports organisations and leadership teams operating in complex environments, with work spanning engineering leadership, executive coaching, systems architecture, and applied research. Engagements are typically long-term, context-specific, and focused on decision-making under real constraints rather than packaged solutions.

JZNSK Ltd also acts as a publishing and custodial entity for research outputs, working papers, frameworks, and professional artefacts produced through its activities. These materials are made available for reference and transparency, and are linked from external publications and associated projects.

JZNSK Ltd is led by Rodrigo Jazinski and operates internationally.
